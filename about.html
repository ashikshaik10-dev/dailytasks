About Massachusetts Institute of Technology

The Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) is one of the world's most prestigious universities, known for excellence in science, engineering, technology, business, and research. It is located in Cambridge, Massachusetts, USA, near Boston.

    Key Facts

  • Founded: 1861
  • Location: Cambridge, Massachusetts, USA
  • Type: Private research university
  • Motto: Mens et Manus ("Mind and Hand")
  • Campus Size: About 168 acres
  • Students: Around 11,800 (undergraduate and graduate)
  • Official Language: English
