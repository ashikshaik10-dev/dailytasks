Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is one of the fastest-growing technologies in the world. It enables machines to think, learn, and make decisions like humans. AI is used in healthcare, education, banking, cybersecurity, robotics, and many other industries.

Applications of Artificial Intelligence

  • Healthcare
  • Cybersecurity
  • Education
  • Banking and Finance
  • Robotics
  • Self-Driving Cars
  • Virtual Assistants

Steps to Learn AI

  1. Learn Programming (Python)
  2. Study Mathematics
  3. Understand Machine Learning
  4. Learn Deep Learning
  5. Practice AI Projects
  6. Build Real-Time Applications
